## Problem

A small service business needs more than a simple contact page. Visitors should understand the brand, see the services, choose an appointment, move naturally into WhatsApp and give the owner a clean way to review activity and availability.

The experience also needs to work well on mobile, because most customers arrive from social links, messages or direct phone browsing.

## Solution

Tiki Tak Barber Studio presents the business through a focused mobile experience: landing page, booking flow, service selection, date/time selection, WhatsApp handoff, app-style screen and admin overview.

The screenshots are public-safe. Private business details such as the full street address were removed from the public presentation.

## Key Features

- Mobile-first landing page with strong brand presentation.
- Service cards, pricing context and booking call-to-action.
- Appointment request flow with selected service, date and time.
- WhatsApp handoff that carries booking details into the conversation.
- PWA-style app screen for a focused customer experience.
- Admin view for appointments, availability and business overview.
- Gallery, FAQ and visual content areas for trust-building.
- Hebrew right-to-left interface designed for local users.
- Public-safe screenshots for GitHub presentation.

## Business Value

The project gives a local business a more polished online presence, reduces repeated explanation in chat, makes booking easier from mobile and creates a clearer path from visitor interest to appointment request.
